Why Japanese clients are withdrawing from India

Spec dependency — cannot fill in what is not written
Japanese software culture relies on tacit understanding. Indian outsourcing models are built on exhaustive written specs. The gap is structural, not personal.

Vietnam and China filling the vacuum on different axes
Vietnam: cost + perceived compliance. China: technical depth in hardware-software integration. India's middle position is the most exposed.

Stream 3 — RCA and technical consulting retainer
